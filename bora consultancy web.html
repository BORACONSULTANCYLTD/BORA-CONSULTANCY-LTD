About Us

BORACONSULTANCY LTD is a leading surveying and consultancy firm located in Kinyinya District. We specialize in providing professional services in land surveying, construction, parcel selling, valuation, house design, and real estate.

Mission: Providing accurate and professional surveying, construction, and real estate solutions.

Vision: Becoming the leading surveying and real estate consultancy in Rwanda.

Core Values: Precision, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Services

  • Gupima ubutaka (Land surveying)
  • Kwemeza imbibi (Boundary confirmation)
  • Gukosora imbibi (Boundary corrections)
  • Gukata ubutaka (Parcel subdivision)
  • Gutera bornes (Placement of boundary markers)
  • Construction and house design
  • Real estate services, including parcel selling and valuation
